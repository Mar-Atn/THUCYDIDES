Discussion Points

  1. Columbia: Map was warm beige — now steel blue (muted). This is a big shift. Blue = western/NATO feel. Or keep the warm beige map and use blue only for UI?
  2. Cathay: Map was pale gold — now dusty red (map) with vivid red (UI). Red = traditional Cathay color. The gold map was pleasant but didn't say "Cathay."
  3. Ruthenia vs Caribe: Both are green in this proposal. Need differentiation — make Caribe more yellow-green or Ruthenia more blue-green?
  4. Sarmatia: Dusty pink/mauve is unusual. Gray would be more "neutral." But gray is boring on the map. Keep the slight warm tone?
  5. Western Treaty countries: Should they share a color FAMILY (blues/teals) to signal alliance, or be maximally distinct for readability?
  6. Phrygia, Mirage, Solaria: All warm brown/copper/gold. Close to each other. Need more separation?
