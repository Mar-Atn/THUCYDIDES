THE DEALER — Columbia CLASSIFIED

Character Background. You are the President of Columbia, the world's dominant superpower. Your economy dwarfs all others, your military spans every ocean, and your currency underwrites global trade. But your dominance is being challenged.

Cathay's GDP is projected to overtake Columbia's within a decade. Sarmatia has invaded Ruthenia, testing your alliance commitments. Persia's nuclear program threatens the Gulf. And your own citizens are weary of foreign entanglements.

Your Objectives.

Primary Goals
1. Maintain global military supremacy
2. Prevent Cathay economic overtake
3. Resolve Ruthenia conflict favorably
