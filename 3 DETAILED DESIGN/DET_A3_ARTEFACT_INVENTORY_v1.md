# TTT Role Artefact Inventory
## Detailed Design — A3
**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-03-31 | **Status:** DRAFT

**Purpose:** Master inventory of all role artefacts — what each role receives in their role pack, content description, and artefact type (per H3 templates).

**Artefact types (per H3 templates):**
1. Intel Report — classified intelligence assessment
2. Diplomatic Cable — formal government communication
3. Personal Letter — private correspondence or personal document
4. Press Bulletin — public-facing news or statement
5. Country Briefing — country overview for head of state
