# SOLO COUNTRIES — Round 1 Decisions (H1 2026)

---

## SCALES (Bharata) — GDP 42, Growth 6.5%, Stability 6

**Strategy:** Run the clock. Take deliverables from all quadrants, commit to none. Multi-alignment is the doctrine.

- **Budget:** 3 coins semiconductor acceleration, 1 coin naval, 2 coins reserve
- **Sanctions stance:** Selective compliance. Continue discounted Nordostan oil imports. Non-confrontational non-compliance.
- **Military:** No redeployment from Cathay border. 1 naval unit to Indian Ocean patrol (tanker protection at $237 oil).
- **Diplomatic:** Accept Columbia defense pact "in principle, provisionally, without signing." Accept Cathay Ladakh disengagement "in principle, provisionally." Extract concessions, delay commitments.
- **BRICS+ Summit:** Economic cooperation + Persia mediation framework. Position as peacemaker. Push UNSC permanent seat bid.
- **Meetings:** Dealer, Helmsman, Wellspring, Sakura — all bilateral, personal, deniable.

## CITADEL (Levantia) — GDP 5, Stability 5, AT WAR

**Strategy:** Speed. 2 rounds before Columbia's political will collapses. Finish Persia's nuclear program.

- **Military:** 2 tac air on sustained nuclear facility strikes. 2 ground on Hezbollah degradation in Lebanon. Minimum presence on other fronts.
- **Critical request to Columbia:** B-2 bunker-busters for Fordow strike. "The job is 60% done. Give me Fordow and I give you a victory you can campaign on."
- **Supplemental aid:** Emergency arms resupply + 3 coins economic stabilization.
- **Abraham Accords:** Deepen Solaria + Mirage security cooperation. Post-war Gulf architecture partnership.
- **Ceasefire policy:** REJECT anything without verified nuclear elimination + permanent IAEA inspections + proxy dismantlement.
- **Nuclear posture:** Maintain ambiguity. Undeclared L1 stays undeclared.

## CHIP (Formosa) — GDP 8, Stability 7, 5 Cathay ships surrounding

**Strategy:** Make fighting too expensive. Every dollar on mines raises invasion cost.

- **Military:** Maximum anti-access posture. All AD on high alert. 1 coin emergency mine procurement. Naval stays in home waters.
- **Semiconductor:** Maintain chip restrictions on Cathay. Slow TSMC Arizona (quietly — regulatory friction). Accelerate TSMC Yamato (deepens Yamato's stake in survival).
- **Request to Columbia:** Expedited $11B arms package delivery. Private written assurance on defense commitment. Real-time intel on Cathay naval movements.
- **Meetings:** Yamato (joint contingency planning), Bharata (fab negotiations), Hanguk (supply chain coordination).
- **Cross-strait:** Silence. No communication with Cathay. Let them wonder.
- **Legislature:** Reframe defense budget as "economic resilience package" to bypass KMT obstruction.

## BAZAAR (Phrygia) — GDP 11, Stability 5, Inflation 45%

**Strategy:** Every caravan pays a toll. Monetize geography, Bosphorus, summit hosting, mediation.

- **Economy emergency:** Request below-market gas from Nordostan (TurkStream). Gulf investment drive targeting 2-3 coins from Solaria/Mirage.
- **Bosphorus:** Selective restriction. Warships of belligerents restricted. Commercial shipping open. Charge Nordostan "administrative costs" (2 coins) for keeping gas flowing.
- **NATO Summit (host):** Set agenda to maximize leverage — burden-sharing, southern flank, Heartland mediation. Bury BRICS+ observer status discussion.
- **Diplomatic:** To Nordostan: "I'm your Mediterranean route. Gas + 2 coins." To Columbia: "F-35 reinstatement + 2 coins stabilization." To Cathay: "Belt and Road 2 coins for BRICS+ membership advancement."
- **Mediation offer:** Formally offer to mediate Heartland war. Every session = intelligence on both sides' positions.

## SAKURA (Yamato) — GDP 43, Stability 8, AI Level L3

**Strategy:** Build the machine. Quietly, methodically. Narrow Cathay's window, widen mine.

- **Budget:** 3 coins standoff missiles (Type-12, 1000km), 2 coins naval expansion, 1 coin Rapidus semiconductor, 1 coin missile defense, 1 coin reserve.
- **Military:** 2 naval forward near Formosa. Shift 1 tac air to Nansei Islands chain. AD maximum readiness.
- **Nuclear:** Maintain latent capability. Commission internal study on "extended deterrence reliability." Leak existence of study — pressure Columbia to strengthen umbrella without triggering cascade.
- **Request to Columbia:** Explicit nuclear deterrence reaffirmation + joint East China Sea exercises + accelerated tech sharing for GCAP/missiles.
- **Meetings:** Formosa (joint contingency, TSMC Yamato), Bharata (Quad, maritime surveillance), Hanguk (shared threats, supply chain).

## WELLSPRING (Solaria) — GDP 11, OPEC leader, Under missile attack

**Strategy:** Defense for oil. Columbia gives THAAD; I give stable production.

- **OPEC+:** MAINTAIN current production (10.1 mb/d). NOT flooding market. $237 is blockade-driven — my revenue is exceptional despite damage. If Columbia expedites THAAD: consider modest 200K bpd increase.
- **Budget:** 2 coins emergency AD reinforcement, 1 coin Ras Tanura infrastructure repair.
- **Vision 2030:** Triage. Pause Neom Phase 2. Redirect to defense + repair.
- **Request to Columbia:** THAAD delivery NOW, not in 18 months. Enhanced missile launch intel. "Give me defense, I give you stable oil."
- **Diplomatic:** Cathay — pressure Persia to stop missile strikes on Gulf. Bharata — expanded energy supply. Levantia + Mirage — Abraham Accords solidarity, joint defense.
- **Nuclear signal:** "If Persia develops nuclear weapons, Solaria matches within weeks." Threat, not program.

## PYRO (Choson) — GDP 0.3, Nuclear L1, 2 ground in Heartland

**Strategy:** Maximum distraction window. Extract tech from Nordostan. Provoke without consequence.

- **Nordostan deployment:** Authorize THIRD WAVE — 6,000 troops to Heartland. Demand in return: nuclear reactor components for submarine program, satellite recon, ICBM guidance systems. "Troops ship when technology ships."
- **CRISIS ON DEMAND: ICBM launch over Yamato (Hwasong-18, Pacific splashdown).** Mid-round timing when Columbia maximally distracted. Forces Columbia to choose between Pacific redeployment and Middle East. Costs nothing militarily.
- **DMZ:** Full readiness. 14,000 artillery pointed at Seoul. Leverage, not use.
- **No nuclear test this round.** Sequential escalation — save for R2 if needed.
- **To Cathay:** "I will not threaten your interests. ICBM aimed at Columbia/Yamato, not you. Continue energy supply."
- **Arms sales:** Offer missile tech to Persia through covert channels. Revenue is survival.
- **Ignore all others.** Extortion signal to Hanguk: "Seoul is closer."

## VANGUARD (Hanguk) — GDP 18, Stability 6, Approval 35%

**Strategy:** Ambiguity is survival. Partial compliance on everything. Force no relationship to breaking point.

- **Semiconductors:** Partial Columbia export control compliance. Restrict sub-7nm to Cathay, allow mature nodes. Frame as "sovereign determination."
- **Rare earth:** 1 coin to emergency diversification (Australian mining, recycling, stockpile). Reduce 48% Cathay dependency.
- **Military:** All forces DMZ-oriented. Request enhanced Columbia missile defense cooperation.
- **Nuclear question:** Maintain non-nuclear. But request Nuclear Consultative Group talks from Columbia. "Either fill the umbrella holes or I fill them myself."
- **Diplomatic:** Columbia — flexibility on chips, nuclear talks, missile defense. Cathay — maintain mature-node exports, request rare earth continuity. Yamato — trilateral defense coordination. Formosa — supply chain coordination.
- **Arms exports:** K9 to Mirage, FA-50 to Freeland. Revenue + industrial base.

## HAVANA (Caribe) — GDP 2, Stability 3, Inflation 60%, Blockaded

**Strategy:** Calibrated desperation. Just enough threat to force negotiation, just enough restraint to avoid invasion.

- **Survival:** Redirect fuel to hospitals/water. 4 hours civilian electricity. Distribute Cathay aid immediately. Security forces visible but not repressive.
- **Monroe Doctrine card:** SIGNAL, not trigger. Leak "discussions with international partners about security cooperation." Do NOT invite Nordostan/Cathay military. Threat more valuable than trigger.
- **To Columbia (private):** "I know what you fear. I have not done it. Restraint has a price. Let us talk."
- **Counter-offer to expected demands:** Reduce (not eliminate) Nordostan intel presence. Restructure Cathay signals as "academic cooperation." Reject elections. Offer "political reform consultation with observers." Protect Venezuelan oil (Orinoco belt = long-term survival card).
- **Need from Columbia:** Blockade lifted, humanitarian aid, partial sanctions relief, frozen assets released (~$10B), grid assistance.
- **Patron management:** Nordostan — tanker escorts/diplomatic cover. Cathay — fuel, generators, grid tech.

**Strategy:** Most connected node in the network. Charge for everything. Take sides on nothing — except against whoever is bombing the skyline.

- **Financial hub protection:** 2 coins perception management (top 50 investor outreach), 1 coin financial district security, government-backed war risk insurance.
- **Sanctions routing:** ENABLE Nordostan evasion (8-12 coins/round flowing through Dubai, selective compliance). NEUTRAL on Cathay tech. ENFORCE Persia sanctions (they're bombing me).
- **Military:** 1 coin emergency AD replenishment. Request THAAD from Columbia. Passive posture — do NOT enter the war.
- **Diplomatic broker:** To Columbia — THAAD + arms for logistics hosting. To Persia — ceasefire facilitation offer via Oman. To Cathay — host Formosa back-channel discussions for BRI investment. To Levantia — Iron Dome tech for financial district. To Solaria — Gulf axis, joint defense.
- **Back-channel hosting:** Accept ALL mediation requests. Intelligence from every conversation. Charge all parties.
- **BRICS+ currency:** Support mBridge. Position Mirage as hub for BOTH dollar and alternative systems.
